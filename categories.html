Pain & Fever Relief

Medications for reducing pain, inflammation, and fever. Includes NSAIDs, acetaminophen, and combination products.

Medication Common Brands Uses Sources
Ibuprofen
Nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drug (NSAID)
Advil Motrin Nuprin
  • Pain relief (headache, toothache, backache)
  • Reduces inflammation
  • Fever reduction
  • Menstrual cramps
Do not exceed 3200mg/day
Drugs.com MedlinePlus Mayo Clinic
Acetaminophen
Analgesic and antipyretic
Tylenol Panadol FeverAll
  • Pain relief
  • Fever reduction
  • Headaches
  • Minor arthritis pain
Max 3000mg/day (4000mg with doctor approval)
Drugs.com MedlinePlus
Naproxen
NSAID (long-lasting)
Aleve Naprosyn Midol Extended Relief
  • Pain relief (lasts 8-12 hours)
  • Arthritis pain
  • Menstrual cramps
  • Muscle aches
Drugs.com MedlinePlus

Important Warnings

  • Do not mix different NSAIDs
  • Avoid alcohol with acetaminophen
  • Consult doctor if pregnant or breastfeeding
  • Stop use if stomach pain occurs

When to See a Doctor

  • Pain lasts more than 10 days
  • Fever lasts more than 3 days
  • Severe stomach pain
  • Signs of allergic reaction

Allergy Relief

Medications for seasonal allergies, hay fever, and allergic reactions. Includes antihistamines, decongestants, and nasal sprays.

Medication Common Brands Uses Sources

Second-generation antihistamine
Claritin Alavert Generic brands
  • Seasonal allergies
  • Hay fever symptoms
  • Hives
  • Non-drowsy formula

Cetirizine
Second-generation antihistamine
Zyrtec Aller-Tec Generic brands
  • Indoor/outdoor allergies
  • Year-round allergies
  • Chronic hives
  • 24-hour relief
Drugs.com MedlinePlus
Fexofenadine
Third-generation antihistamine
Allegra Fexidine Generic brands
  • Seasonal allergic rhinitis
  • Chronic idiopathic urticaria
  • Non-drowsy formula
  • Works within 1 hour
Drugs.com MedlinePlus

Usage Tips

  • Take before allergy season starts
  • Avoid taking with fruit juices
  • Some may cause drowsiness
  • Check for drug interactions

Allergy Types

  • Seasonal (pollen, grass)
  • Perennial (dust, pets)
  • Food allergies
  • Skin allergies
